About Us

Our website was started as a micro project in Govt Polytechnic Himmatnagar, Gujarat, India. It was created by four students: Dev Joshi, Vanzara Tirth, Patel Raj, and Patel Preyash, as part of their coursework in the subject "Static Web Page Design" during the second semester of the Computer Engineering branch. The project was suggested by Faculty Nirav Sir with the aim of learning and applying the knowledge gained.
